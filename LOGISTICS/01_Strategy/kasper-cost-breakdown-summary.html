01 — Per-device cost, mid-tier

What one Tier 2 device costs to deploy and run

Two cost types, kept separate: capex — spent once, at install — and recurring — spent every month the device stays active. Data/SIM cost is confirmed at AED 120/device/year (AED 10/month).

All-in Year 1 cost per device: AED 731 — AED 533 capex + AED 198 in recurring cash costs over the first 12 months. Against Tier 2's AED 1,060 Day-1 collection (AED 960 subscription + AED 100 install fee), that's a Year 1 cash profit of AED 329/device, before the Year 2 renewal — which costs only AED 198 (no capex) against AED 960 revenue.
Platform cost is excluded from this figure — and so is hosting/dev cost. The tracking platform itself (Wialon/Navixy per-device fee, or a custom-built alternative) is not in this table because it's still unconfirmed. Two things are missing entirely from this cost model and need to be closed before it's final: (1) the Wialon/Navixy per-device platform quote — every AED 1/device shifts total cost meaningfully at 424 devices, and (2) AWS/hosting cost and one-time development cost if a custom platform is built instead — dev cost has no estimate yet at all, and AWS hosting is only a rough range (AED 92–147/mo at 100 devices) in the feasibility study, not confirmed.

02b — Capital required including one full year of data

Hardware + install + 12 months of SIM, not just activation

Section 01 separated capex (one-time) from recurring cost (monthly). But if the planning question is "how much cash do we need to put a device in the field and keep its data connection alive for a full year" — the right unit is hardware + installation + 12 months of SIM, not just the AED 25 one-time activation fee.
