The Problem — What UAE Fleet Operators Cannot Do Today
🔧  Breakdowns are always surprises
Vendors have no early warning system. A truck breaks down mid-job: emergency repair AED 30–80K, client job delayed, contract penalty. All predictable with engine hour data — but nobody has that data today.
🚛  Deadhead trips waste 40% of capacity
A truck delivers to Fujairah and drives back empty. 40% of UAE construction fleet kilometres are dead runs. AI dispatch can match a return load in real time and convert that dead run into revenue.
🌿  ESG reporting is impossible
UAE sustainability mandates and KSA Vision 2030 require CO2 per equipment type per hour. Enterprise clients need this for regulatory compliance. Nobody can provide it today without telematics data.
📊  Nobody knows what freight rates should be
There is no published UAE freight rate index by lane. Rates are quoted by feel. Freight forwarders, insurers, and developers have no benchmark. Kasper will own this data after 10,000 jobs.
🎥  Insurance is overpriced because drivers are unscored
Logistics SMEs pay full fleet insurance with no proof of driver behaviour. AI dashcam scoring with incident detection can cut premiums 10–20%. Insurance companies will fund the hardware to get the data.
⚡  Surge demand is invisible — rates do not adjust
During the Hormuz crisis, land freight demand surged 733%. Rates did not adjust in real time. With dynamic pricing on a live supply-demand signal, Kasper captures that upside automatically.
